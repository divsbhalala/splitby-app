# Splitting Travel Expenses with Friends

Traveling with friends creates unforgettable memories — but untangling the shared costs afterward can turn joy into headaches. Splitby makes it effortless to track, split, and settle travel expenses so you can focus on the adventure.

## The travel expense tangle

Group trips layer expenses across flights, lodging, food, transport, and activities. Who paid what, when, and for whom becomes a spreadsheet nightmare. Splitby replaces manual tracking with real-time clarity.

## Core features for travelers

- **Multi-currency support**: Record expenses in local currencies; Splitby converts using the payment date rates so totals stay accurate.
- **Group wallets**: Create trip-specific wallets to isolate expenses from your everyday groups.
- **Expense templates**: Save recurring trip items (breakfast, gas, tolls) as templates for one-tap entry.
- **Photo receipts**: Snap and attach photos; OCR pulls amount, vendor, and date automatically.
- **Offline mode**: Log expenses without signal; syncs when you reconnect.
- **Auto-settle**: See who owes whom and settle with in-app payment links or export to PayPal/Venmo.

## Step-by-step: Tracking a trip

1. **Create a trip wallet**: Name it (e.g., "Japan 2026") and add travelers.
2. **Set base currency**: Choose your settlement currency (USD, EUR, etc.).
3. **Log expenses on the fly**: Amount, currency, payer, split type (equal, shares, percent), and category.
4. **Attach receipts**: Optional but recommended for disputes.
5. **Review balances daily**: Dashboard shows running balances and settlement totals.
6. **Settle up**: Export balances or use Splitby Pay to close debts in one tap.

## Pro tips to keep peace

- **Settle mid-trip**: Small payments prevent end-of-trip sticker shock.
- **Use categories**: Tag meals, transport, lodging for easy cost breakdowns.
- **Agree on rules early**: Split accommodation equally? Food per person? Transport by usage? Decide before you go.
- **Backup exports**: Download CSV at trip end for personal records.
